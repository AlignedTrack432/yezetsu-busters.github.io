Meet the person behind the revolution:


JustPudding



The fearless leader of the YZB


They aren’t replying to my Discord messages so I’ll just wing it.

Just_Pudding is a 903 year old sugarcane farmer, and the leader of this war. They were born in the fields of Milwaukee, Wisconsin in 1123, and raised in a rather high-class family. However, when they were just 8 years old, their parents tragically died in a car accident.

This crushed Just_Pudding, but inspired them to keep going, in honour of them. By 15, they were already a highly skilled craftsman, and by 20, they owned a hugely successful business.

At 23 years old, they were wandering through nature, looking for inspiration for new products, and unbeknownst to them, touched a cursed amulet, granting them immortality and invincibility.

They decided to use this power to fight crimes for many years, until they reached 508, where they decided to end their career, and retire and relax.

Things were peaceful, they took up pottery and worked in the sugarcane farms in their free time, until they came accross a VTuber named Yezetsu.

At first, everything was fine, until one unsuspecting stream, a viewer came in and tried to redeem the reward for Discord access, and got denied.

Just_Pudding, recognizing how unacceptable this discrimination was, decided to re-enter crime fighting, and started the YZB.

Now, they fight tooth and nail to give viewers the freedom they deserve.
