About

We are the YZB, a foundation dedicated to exposing Yezetsu and his crimes, and helping to give equality to Twitch viewers everywhere.

In our hour of need, when Yezetsu refused to give out multiple invite links, Just_Pudding stepped up, and took the beginning steps in founding what we now know as the YZB.

However, things were not all well. Yezetsu theatened Just__Pudding with bans, and even timed out their right hand, deleting all the messages they had sent in the process.

However, we did not give up, and we never will. In fact, this only worked as fuel, and so, the YZB was finally created.

We engaged in peaceful protests, losing over six thousand channel points in the process. However, this only motivated the YZB to keep going.

Eventually, Just_Pudding was instated as a mod on the Discord server. Nobody knows what Yezetsu has planned.

Just_Pudding, being the incredible leader they are, decided to finally give out the Discord links we needed, and the Discord links we deserved.

Our battle is not over, and until Yezetsu is taken down, it never will be. But as dawn breaks for another day, we keep fighting like we always have.

Yours truly, YZB.
